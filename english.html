My Courses

In the ten years that I have lived in Great Britain, I have developed a great love for the English language, which I would like to share with others who need it. Sometimes I have fixed times and courses for this, and sometimes I tailor the lessons to the needs of the pupils / students. I have experience with pre-schoolers, primary school students, teenagers and adults. Lessons can be one-off or consist of longer-term supervision of students and others. A short course for adults and / or children who move abroad (expat course) is also possible. New are a 2- to 6-year course for primary school students in preparation for bilingual secondary education and a course for job seekers. You will soon find information about this on this website. Target price per hour: € 10 - € 50 depending on the number of participants and level of the course.

English for Life

Description:
Especially for job seekers - Elementary or Intermediate. At a time when internationalization is rampant, a good knowledge of the English language is often a precondition for finding a new job. And even if you do not immediately need this knowledge for your work, it is still good for a potential new employer to know that you are investing in yourself.
In the English for Life Intermediate course, or if you prefer to start at a somewhat lower level Elementary, we will go through the principles of English. A balanced program with an emphasis on your needs. Vocabulary, idioms and grammar are all covered with a lot of attention to speaking skills. The course is given in groups of five to ten students. We will start as soon as five students have registered.
Lesson times: 2x a week for two hours during ten weeks on Tuesdays and Fridays from 9.30 - 11.30.

Costs: depending on the chosen location: 500-520 euros for the course including materials.

Twelve course for children

Description:
TWELVE course for children aged - you guessed it - around twelve years old. Are you now in group 7 and do you want to go to bilingual education after primary school? Or are you in group 8 and do you want to be well prepared for secondary school? Or do you have trouble with English in seventh grade and want to do something about it?
During the summer holidays you have the opportunity to do so: two weeks at the beginning or three weeks at the end of the holiday you can participate in an intensive training where you will always be taught in a group for two hours in the morning.

Lesson times: 2x a week for two hours during ten weeks on Tuesdays and Fridays from 9.30 - 11.30.

Costs: 250 euros for the course, including materials. Location: Arnhem South or De Beukennoot Heteren
