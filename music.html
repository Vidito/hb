MusicLessons

At 'De Beukennoot Muziek' you can take recorder lessons. These lessons can be filled in in many ways, from children who come in groups to someone who has been playing for a long time and wants to know more about a specific topic.
Besides recorder lessons, theory courses are also given. Here you learn to read notes (useful if you play an instrument but you have never learned to read music or if you want to master the art without having to spend hours behind an instrument), everything about keys and rhythms and the principles of harmony . For this I use the material from the ABRSM: Music Theory in Practice , which I have translated - with permission - for use by Dutch speakers.
Prices : From € 14 for a child group lesson to € 30 for an individual adult. For information on current prices email: henriettejbos AT gmail.com.
