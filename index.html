About The Instructor

As a music teacher: After Henriëtte graduated from the Maastricht Conservatory in 1989, she taught for a number of years in Gelderland and Overijssel, both at home and at music schools. Between 1996 and 2006 she lived in and around Edinburgh. There she taught music at home at the George Watson's College Music school, St Mary's Music school, and Loretto's (Musselburgh). Besides teaching children and adults to play the recorder, she taught the Edinburgh SRP ( Society of Recorder Players ) conducted.
As a music teacher: After returning from Great Britain, she studied English teacher training and taught at various secondary schools for several years. In September 2010 she founded De Beukennoot Muziek in Heteren.
